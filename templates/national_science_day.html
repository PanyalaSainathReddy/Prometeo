INDIA's Journey in Science and Technology

Prof. R. Chidambaram

Director of The Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC)

Indian Physicist who is known for his integral role in India's nuclear weapons program, he coordinated test preparation for the Pokhran-I (1975) and Pokhran-II (1998). Previously served as the principal scientific adviser to the federal Government of India, and also served as the director of the Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC) and later as chairman, Atomic Energy Commission of the Government of India and he contributed in providing national defence and energy security to India. Prof. Samir Brahmachari

Ex-DG CSIR

Indian biophysicist and Former Director General of the Council of Scientific & Industrial Research(CSIR) and Former Secretary, Department of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR), Government of India. He is the Founder Director of CSIR - Institute of Genomics and Integrative Biology (IGIB), New Delhi and the Chief Mentor of Open Source for Drug Discovery (OSDD) Project. He is the recipient of J.C Bose Fellowship Award, DST (2012).

Prof. Partha Majumder

National Science Chair Professor

Founder of the National Institute of Biomedical Genomics. He has provided immense contributions in the field of genomics and development of statistical methods in understanding genetic basis of human disease.

Prof. Ashutosh Sharma

Ex-Secretary, Department of Science and Technology

Prof. Ashutosh Sharma is an Institute Chair Professor and C V Seshadri Chair Professor at the Department of chemical engineering of Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur.He is best known for his pioneering research work in the areas of colloids, thin film, interfaces, adhesion, patterning and in the fabrication and application of self-assembled nano-structures.
